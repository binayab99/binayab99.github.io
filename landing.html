Welcome to Guitar Band!

Guitar Band Music School offers comprehensive guitar lessons that start with the fundamentals, including scales, chords, tuning, arpeggios, and rhythm adapted to various skill levels.

Our core philosophy is that the best way for students to gain musical proficiency is through performance-based music education. All of our lessons for guitar students include a performance aspect.

Music Classes

Guitar for Beginners

Our teachers will gladly teach you the basics of the world’s most popular instrument.

Rhythm Guitar

Lead Guitar

Blues Guitar

These classes will introduce you to the blues, an incredibly important style of music.

Guitar Theory

About Us

Our guitar school was founded in 1999 to help students of all ages and skill levels to pursue their dream of becoming guitar players. If you would like to learn how to play the world’s most popular instrument, our school is the place for you, regardless of your age and social status. We offer group and private lessons for children and adults.
